Liberica


  • Used for Kapeng Barako or instant coffee
  • Bold, smoky flavor
  • Rare and mostly found in Indonesia or the Philippines


    • While they might be rare, Liberica coffee beans can pack a punch. Liberica is native to the central and western region of Africa and is known for its bold, smoky flavor. Since these coffee beans require hot, humid climates to grow, they are unable to grow in mass quantities.
